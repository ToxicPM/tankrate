Is LPG Right For You?

LPG (Liquefied Petroleum Gas) is one of the cheapest fuels available in the United Kingdom, typically costing 40-50% less per litre than petrol. Most petrol vehicles can be converted to run on LPG using a professional conversion kit costing £1,500-£3,000.

For drivers covering more than 8,000 miles per year, the fuel savings usually offset the conversion cost within 3-5 years. LPG also produces fewer harmful emissions, making it better for the environment.
